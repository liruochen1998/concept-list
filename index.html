Concept List A HackNC 2019 project

We don't like to remember concepts, and we know you don't neither. Thus, we invite you to play this game to help you remember hard concepts. Let's get started...

User Manual
  • Click "add" to add concepts and corresponding definitions
  • Click "delete" to get rid of concepts
  • Click "hide" to hide the right half of the sheet to quickly test yourself
  • Click "show" to reveal the hidden right-half definitions
  • Click "game" to play the game that we presented!

After adding concepts, please click the game button, enjoy the game!
